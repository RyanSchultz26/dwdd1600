Terminology of Body Weight Athletics

Misc. Terminology by Exercise type

Rock Climbing

  • Beta: Information or strategy about a climbing route.
  • Crimp: A small handhold gripped with fingertips.
  • Flash: Completing a route on the first attempt with prior knowledge.

Calisthenics

  • Rep: A single repetition of an exercise (e.g., one pull-up).
  • Set: A group of consecutive reps.
  • Muscle-up: A move combining a pull-up and dip to get above a bar.

Body Weight

  • Plank: A core exercise holding a push-up position.
  • Burpee: A full-body move combining a squat, plank, and jump.
  • Push-up: A chest and arm exercise lowering and raising the body with arms.
