What is High-Protein

The high-protein diet is a nutrition plan with increased protein intake, often used to support muscle growth and weight management. It includes protein-rich foods and supplements like protein powders.

What is a high-protein diet?

A "high-protein" diet is named for its focus on protein as a primary nutrient. It provides an alternative approach to fueling your body when carbohydrate intake is balanced or reduced.

When you eat sufficient protein, your body uses amino acids from foods and powders to build muscle and other tissues. These amino acids serve as a key component throughout the body, especially for muscles.

Muscles are protein-hungry tissues that require consistent amino acid supply, and protein powders offer a convenient source. Muscles rely on protein for repair and growth.

On a high-protein diet, your body optimizes protein use for muscle maintenance and growth. When carbohydrate levels are moderated, muscle-building can become more efficient.
