Fitness Craft

What makes Ryan's Gym so effective? It's his commitment to uncompromising quality and fitness craft. It is strengthening the body and energizing the town with transformative workout experiences.

Ryan began training when he was just 15 years old. He first offered fitness tips from a small booth at local events in Buena Vista.

In 2000, he rented a space and opened the first gym. He developed a full range of workout programs, perfecting every routine and designing each session for maximum results.

This gym has been voted the second best in Buena Vista for the last three years. Locals love what Ryan can do with fitness equipment, and he teaches classes on Thursday nights showing how to replicate the gym experience at home.
