Available Therapies

  • Red Light Therapy: Stimulates cellular energy production, improves blood flow, and reduces inflammation using low-level red and near-infrared light. Enhances recovery and skin rejuvenation. Learn More
  • Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT): Involves breathing 100% oxygen in a pressurized chamber to increase oxygen delivery to tissues, promoting faster healing of wounds and reducing inflammation. Learn More
  • Sensory Deprivation Tank: Float in a soundproof, light-free tank filled with Epsom salt water to reduce stress, enhance relaxation, and improve mental clarity.
  • Cryotherapy: Exposes the body to extremely cold temperatures to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and accelerate muscle recovery after workouts.
  • Infrared Sauna: Uses infrared light to heat the body directly, promoting detoxification, relaxation, and improved circulation.

Why Choose Our Therapies?

Our therapies complement your fitness routine by accelerating recovery, reducing pain, and enhancing overall health. Each session is supervised by trained professionals in a comfortable, spa-like environment.
