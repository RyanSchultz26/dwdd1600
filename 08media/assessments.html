Health Assessments

Airborne Fitness provides advanced health assessments to monitor your progress and optimize your wellness journey.

Available Assessments

  • DEXA Scan: Measures bone density, body fat percentage, and lean muscle mass with high-precision X-ray technology. Learn More
  • Comprehensive Blood Panel: Analyzes cholesterol, glucose, and other key markers to assess cardiovascular health and metabolic function.
  • Hormone Profile Test: Evaluates levels of cortisol, testosterone, and other hormones to optimize energy, recovery, and overall health.
  • VO2 Max Test: Measures aerobic capacity and cardiovascular fitness to tailor your workout intensity and endurance training.
  • Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR) Test: Determines your baseline calorie burn to create personalized nutrition and weight management plans.
  • Blood Micronutrient Test: Assesses vitamin and mineral levels to identify deficiencies and guide dietary adjustments for optimal performance.

Why Assessments Matter

Our assessments provide precise data to track your progress, identify health risks, and customize your fitness and nutrition plans. Conducted by trained professionals, these tests ensure accuracy and actionable insights.
